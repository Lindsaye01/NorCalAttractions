About Northern California Attractions

We believe that we can look to our natural surroundings to find inspiration, and from which we can garner hope, tranquility and the child-like feeling of wonder. But the north state is much more than justy an explorer's great playground. Northern California is the home of much of the west-coast's historical, social and ecological history, as well.

This website is dedicated to highlighting this heart-pounding and awe-inspiring beauty, as well as pointing out the many culturally exciting activities that are offered here all year long. Whether you are a parent wanting to take your children to learn something new that will excite them, such a gold-panning or golf-frisbee, or a native to our gorgeous mountain tops and secluded-waterfalls, we have something to exciting to share with everyone!

How to Navigate Our Site

  1. You may choose one of the monthly "Editor's Choice" articles to read and consider for your next trip. Each article that is highlighted here is something specific to that month, and can be done immediatley.
  2. If you already know of a specific attraction you are interested in, then you go straight to the "Attractions" tab, and all attractions are listed in alphabetical order.
  3. You may also choose the "City" tab in the menu and then navigate to the city you are interested in and choose something to do.
